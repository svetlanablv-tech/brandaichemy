Product Choice
Diagnosis

B2B · Catalogues · Technical categories · Amazon-heavy brands · Distributors

When the data is there but buyers still can't decide, the problem isn't missing information — it's broken choice logic. We find where product data, filters, comparison structures, and card logic prevent buyers from making a confident decision.

This service is built for brands where product complexity is high: many SKUs, technical specifications, variants, compatibility matrices, or multi-channel catalogue management. We use a simple diagnostic formula: data gap → choice failure → silence → lost revenue.

We calculate the commercial cost of that silence — how much manual intervention it generates, how many decisions are abandoned, and what it means for revenue.

Questions

Common questions

I'm not sure which service I need. What should I do?

Start with a conversation. Describe what isn't working — traffic without conversion, a brand that sounds generic, product data that doesn't help buyers decide — and we'll tell you which entry point makes sense. Most clients begin with a diagnosis.

How long does each service take?

Brand and Product Diagnoses typically take 5–10 business days. Choice Architecture takes 3–6 weeks depending on scope. Full Implementation timelines are agreed individually and typically range from 6 to 14 weeks.

Do you work with brands outside the UK?

Yes. We're based in London but work internationally — primarily with brands in the UK, US, Canada, and Europe. All engagements are conducted remotely.

Can you guarantee results?

We can guarantee clarity — a precise diagnosis of where choice breaks down and a documented system to rebuild it. We can't guarantee specific revenue outcomes, because too many factors beyond our scope affect them. What we can tell you is that brands with clear, convincing, AI-readable positioning convert better. That's what we build.
