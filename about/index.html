Founder

Lana Belova

Founder & Brand Architect

Lana has spent over a decade working inside the commercial logic of e-commerce — not on the edges of it. She started selling on Amazon in 2010, built products that ranked and converted, and experienced first-hand what it means to have a business built entirely on a platform you don't control.

In 2018 she founded Ecomlana, which grew into a strategy and education practice through client demand rather than marketing. She has worked with brands across Amazon, Shopify, Etsy, and direct-to-consumer channels — 14 brands, 7 published case studies.

BrandAIchemy is the practice she built for the specific transition she knows from the inside: from Amazon dependency to D2C independence, with AI visibility as the operating principle from day one.

How I work

Three things I don't
compromise on.

01

Strategy before execution

I don't start with deliverables. I start with understanding — where you are, what's built, what's working, what isn't. The work comes after the diagnosis, not before it.

Organic and LLM visibility compounds over months, not weeks. I tell you that upfront — and I show you the trajectory, not just the peak number. A new domain at month 1 with 13 LLM visits is not the same story as zero.

03

One relationship

You work with me directly. No account managers, no handoffs to a junior team. The person who designs the strategy is the person who implements it and answers for the result.
