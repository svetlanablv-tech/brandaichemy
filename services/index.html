Questions

Common questions

I'm not sure which one I need — Rebuild or Build?

Simple rule: if you have a site, that's Rebuild. If you don't, that's Build. If you have a site but it's never been indexed or driven any traffic, it's closer to Build. Start with a conversation — describe what you have and we'll tell you which road makes sense.

How long until I see results?

Rebuild: first organic and LLM signals in 3–4 months. Build: stable traffic in 6–12 months. Both compound — they grow over time, not in a straight line. I show you the trajectory, not just the peak number. Month 1 with 13 LLM visits is not the same story as zero.

Do you guarantee results?

I guarantee the work. A correctly structured, AI-readable brand will be found — that's structural, not probabilistic. I can't control how fast search engines index or how quickly LLMs reference a new source. What I can tell you: the brands built this way don't go backwards.

Why no paid ads or conversion optimisation?

Because mixing organic foundation work with paid traffic obscures what's working. CRO and email belong to specialists who focus on them — I stay in my lane. The result is cleaner, more attributable, and doesn't depend on ad spend to sustain.

Do you work with brands outside the UK?

Yes. Based in London, working remotely with brands in the UK, US, Canada, and Europe.
